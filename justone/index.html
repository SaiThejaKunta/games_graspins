How to play

  1. The active player picks a number from 1 to 5 without seeing the card. Everyone else sees the five words.
  2. The active player closes their eyes. Everyone else writes a one-word clue on their easel.
  3. Compare clues. Any identical or invalid clues are canceled (hide those easels).
  4. The active player opens their eyes and makes one guess from the remaining clues, or passes.
  5. Tap the result in the app. After 13 cards, check your score. Try to beat your record.

Wrong guess penalty

A wrong guess loses two cards: this one plus the next in the deck (or a previously won card if the deck is empty).
