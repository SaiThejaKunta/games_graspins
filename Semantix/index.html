How to Play

1
Guess any word. There's a secret word chosen every day — your goal is to find it.
2
Check the similarity score. After each guess, you see how semantically close your word is. Higher = closer in meaning. Negative = very different.
3
Use proximity. If your guess is in the top closest words, you'll see its rank and a colored bar.
4
Latest guess on top. Below it, all previous guesses are sorted by similarity so you can track your progress.
💡
Need help? Use the Hint button to reveal a word with a better score than your current best.
